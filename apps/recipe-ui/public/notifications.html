Notifications

Stay in the loop with your cooking circle.

Why turn them on

Notifications are how ReciFriend keeps you connected to what your friends are cooking. We only send a handful — never spam.

  • Friend requests — know the moment someone wants to connect.
  • Recipes shared with you — when a friend sends a recipe your way, see it right away.
  • Saves on your recipes — find out when a friend bookmarks something you posted.
  • Friend activity highlights — occasional nudges about what your circle is cooking this week.

How to manage

Notification permissions are managed by your device's system settings. To turn them on or off:

iOS: Settings → ReciFriend → Notifications.
Android / web: use your browser or system settings.

You can revoke permissions any time. We'll never send marketing emails or push notifications.
