ReciFriend

Recipes you save, shared with the people you cook with.

What it is

ReciFriend is a recipe-saving and sharing app for home cooks. Save the recipes you love, share them with friends, and see what the people you cook with are making.

Who it's for

Made for home cooks sharing with family and friends — not influencers, not audiences. If you've ever sent a recipe link in a group chat and wished it lived somewhere better, ReciFriend is for you.

How it works

Paste a link to a TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Pinterest, Allrecipes, or NYT Cooking recipe — or use the iOS share sheet — and we'll extract the ingredients and steps automatically. Add friends, share what you're cooking, and let the people you eat with inspire your next meal.

Support & help

Have a question, hit a bug, or want to suggest a feature? Email hello@recifriend.com. We read every message and usually reply within a day or two.

Common things people ask about:

  • A recipe didn't auto-fill. Open the recipe, tap Edit, then tap Auto-fill to try again — some videos need a second pass once captions load.
  • I want a recipe to be private. Open the recipe, tap the three-dot menu, and uncheck Make public. Private recipes never appear in friends' feeds or on the public discover surface.
  • I want to delete my account. Email hello@recifriend.com from the address you signed up with and we'll erase your account and recipes.
  • Only certain link sources work. Supported right now: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Pinterest, Allrecipes, and NYT Cooking. We restrict source hosts so a saved-and-shared recipe can't be used to lead friends to a phishing or malware page.
